Company Overview

Building Malaysia's infrastructure with quality granite

Teik Granite Quarry Sdn Bhd (3821-P) was established on 20 June 1960 and is a reputable quarry and mining company based in George Town, Penang, Malaysia.

We operate in the quarry and mining industry, specializing in granite extraction and supply, earth materials, sand, and aggregates supply for construction and infrastructure projects.

Our materials are used in road construction, building projects, and infrastructure works across Malaysia. With over six decades of experience, we are committed to quality, reliability, and sustainable quarrying practices.
