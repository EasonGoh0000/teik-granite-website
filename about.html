Company History

Teik Granite Quarry Sdn Bhd was established on 20 June 1960 and is a reputable quarry and mining company based in George Town, Penang, Malaysia. We are registered under the registration number 196001000153 (3821-P).

We operate in the quarry and mining industry, specializing in:

  • Granite extraction and supply
  • Earth materials supply
  • Sand and aggregates supply

Our materials are used in road construction, building projects, and infrastructure works across Malaysia. With over six decades of experience, we are committed to quality, reliability, and sustainable quarrying practices.

Teik Granite Group comprises five subsidiary companies that work together to provide comprehensive quarry and construction material solutions.
